Our Story

From the Heart of Khorasan

Dokhte Watan — "Daughter of the Homeland" — is a one-woman effort to keep the living culture of the Tajiks of Khorasan visible, wearable, and celebrated.

Every piece is handmade. The embroidery, the cuts, the colors — all rooted in the traditions of Afghan Tajiks, a people whose culture is too often overlooked or erased.

This isn't just fashion. It's resistance, memory, and love — worn on the body.
